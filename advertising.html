No, the management fee is separate from your ad spend. You pay the ad platforms directly. I recommend a minimum monthly ad spend of $6k to see real results.

Setup takes 5-7 business days days per advertising channel. While initial data comes in immediately, optimisation for peak ROAS typically takes 1-2 months for Meta & 2-3 months for Google as the algorithms learn.

I provide creative strategy, briefs, and feedback. For actual asset production (video/photo), I can refer you to partners or work with your existing team.
